I'm a second year Ph.D student at [Georgia Tech](https://www.gatech.edu/) advised by Prof. [Faramarz Fekri](https://fekri.ece.gatech.edu/). My ressearch primarily primarily focuses on the intersection of Probabilistic graphical models and compressed sensing. In particular, I'm interested in understanding the feasibility of recovering the graph structure from compressed observational data. Previously, I got my Masters from Georgia Tech and I did my undergraduate studies at National Institute of Technology, Trichy [(NITT)](https://www.nitt.edu/). 

In my free time I like to do a bit of cooking and explore hiking trails around Atlanta.
