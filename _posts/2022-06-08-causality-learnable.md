<p style='text-align: justify;'> Establishing causal relations is one of the main ways by which we develop an understanding of the world around us. Some of the successful laws of science, Newton's second law for example, do precisely this. In this case, the force exerted causally influences the movement of an object, or more concisely, \( \boldsymbol{f} = m\boldsymbol{a} \). </p>
